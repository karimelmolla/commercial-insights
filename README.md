Business Transaction Data Analysis (July – December 2024)
Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing business transaction data from July to December 2024 using completed transactions only. The main objective was to extract actionable business insights related to merchant performance, city contribution, category profitability, and promotional opportunities.

The analysis simulates a real-world business case where data is used to support strategic decision-making, improve marketing efforts, and identify growth opportunities.

Key Business Questions Answered
Top 5 Merchants by GMV per Quarter
Identified the highest-performing merchants based on Gross Merchandise Value (GMV) for each quarter.
Compared merchant performance trends over time.
City Contribution to Total GMV
Analyzed monthly GMV contribution by city.
Compared city performance across selected months to identify changes in customer activity and demand.
Category Expansion Recommendation
Recommended merchant categories for expansion using:
Total GMV
Profit Margin (fees_egp - cost_egp)
Evaluated which categories offer the strongest growth and profitability potential.
Promotion & Budget Recommendation
Selected merchants for promotional campaigns based on GMV ranking, seasonal demand, and transaction performance.
Recommended the best month for each promotion opportunity.
Additional Business Insights
Identified trends, seasonal spikes, high-performing cities, and merchant behavior patterns.
Suggested actionable recommendations to improve business performance.

The purpose of this project is to demonstrate how transactional business data can be transformed into meaningful insights that support data-driven decision-making and business growth strategies.
